Naserian Kasura CEO & Founder of Tenebo Foundation.

This Foundation exists because every life lost to TB is a life too many. We are committed to making a difference. Through research, innovative initiatives, awareness, and support, we can break the cycle of TB and create a healthier future."

Tenebo Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to eradicating Tuberculosis in Kenya and Africa as a whole.The organziation was founded in January 2024 by Naserian Kasura, who was diagnosed with tuberculosis in August 2022.Drawing from her journey, she started this organization dedicated to tackling TB at its core.

Read More

Our mission is to empower communities most affected by tuberculosis through education, awareness and access to prevention, diagnosis and treatment services ultimately paving the way for a TB-free country and continent.

Want to be a volunteer?

Are you ready to make a difference?


Join our team in the fight against tuberculosis!
By becoming a volunteer, you can contribute to a brighter future and a healthier world.

At our organization, we believe in the power of teamwork and compassion. Together, we can provide support to those affected by tuberculosis, offering hope and healing to the vulnerable, the marginalized, and the voiceless.

Volunteering with us comes with its rewards:

  • Training to equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to assist TB patients.
  • An opportunity to make a meaningful impact in the lives of individuals battling tuberculosis, especially children from underserved communities.
  • Your willingness to help is what matters most.
