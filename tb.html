What is Tuberculosis?

Tuberculosis (TB) is a bacterial infection caused by Mycobacterium tuberculosis. It primarily affects the lungs but can also affect other parts of the body.

Symptoms of TB

Common symptoms of TB include persistent cough, fever, weight loss, night sweats, and coughing up blood. However, not everyone infected with TB bacteria becomes sick.

fr

Prevention and Treatment

Preventive measures include maintaining good hygiene, avoiding close contact with TB patients, and getting vaccinated if available. Treatment involves a combination of antibiotics taken over several months.

Tenebo Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to eradicating Tuberculosis in Kenya and Africa as a whole.
